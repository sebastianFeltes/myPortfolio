SEBASTIAN FELTES

Web developer

Imagination has no limits...

Hi there! I am a 32 yo Argentine Web Developer who is currently learning different technologies for the development of web pages and mobile and desktop applications. I consider myself a curious and creative person, responsible and committed. I feel that through web design and within the world of technology itself, there are no set limits. "The sky is the limit" said Christopher Wallace and I think that phrase applies perfectly to this profession. Learning how the world works is the first step to improve it, and technology is a very powerful tool when it comes to helping people and making their lives easier. That's why I like to learn, to be able, in the future, to create useful things for others...

Inspiration? Let me see...

One of my "idols" or references is Aaron Swartz, the so-called "son of the internet": His fight, intelligence and way of seeing the world is an example to follow for all of us who like technology and want a real change in the world. His ideas of sharing knowledge and that every human being has access to all the information available on the ENTIRE network to be able to study and improve their skills are, in my opinion, a fundamental basis for technological and human progress.

Another person who inspires me to keep going is, and don't you dare laugh, the "cook", a fictional character (based on a real person) from the movie "Men of Honor". I saw the movie as a young man and Brashear's tenacity and how he overcomes adversity and "what will they say" is impressive. I come from a slum in the province of Buenos Aires, where, unfortunately, thanks to the possibilities, there are few people who can aspire to be "great" or "do great things", which is why I always looked for ways to stand out and seek my place in the world to show that no matter where you come from, everyone is responsible for their destiny...
